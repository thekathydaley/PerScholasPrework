My education

At 26 years old, I decided to push myself and see what I could do so I started at Clayton State in 2012 and graduated with my Bachelor's in English in 2016 while working full time, taking care of four kids, and pregnant with my youngest son....it didn't end there. I went on to get my Master's in Liberal Arts - English in 2019 as well.

My favorite places to walk

When you combine free stuff and nature, I am always happy. I love walking trails and here are a couple of my favorites! The easiest answer for me is the park near my house: JP Moseley. This park is very convenient but it is also over 2 miles long and has a beautiful stream just off of the path. One of my other favorites is High Falls. This one has beautiful waterfalls and has so many options for hiking. It's beautiful, peaceful, and allows you to spend as much time as you please without getting bored. Walking is the easiest and most enjoyable of exercise options and the trails make it even better!
